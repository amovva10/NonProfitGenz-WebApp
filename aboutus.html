Who We Are

We are a youth-led organization calling to bring awareness to the human rights issues plaguing our society today. Through tutoring/mentorship services, we will donate 100% of our proceeds to organizations that support the Black Lives Matter movement, and nonprofits addressing COVID-19 related problems in hard-hit areas.

Our Team

Akshita Movva

Co-Founder

Akshita earned her Bachelor of Science degree in Biology from Meredith College. She aspires to become a physician and integrate her passions for medicine, education, and social justice while working toward building a society that is more equitable. As a proactive step towards this vision, Akshita co-founded "Gen Z for BLM," an organization aimed at increasing awareness about contemporary human rights issues and advancing social equality to combat injustice in our society.

Aishwarya Movva

Co-Founder

Aishwarya is currently an undergraduate student at Meredith College studying Computer Science. She aims to demonstrate unity with other people of color who are confronting similar issues as a member of a minority group, herself. As a co-founder of Gen Z For BLM, Aishwarya hopes to establish a safe space for marginalized communities to come together and accomplish the change we need in this generation.

Shaye Kibara

Co-Founder

Shaye is currently an undergraduate student at Boston University majoring in Biology. Her goal in the future is to combine her passion for healthcare and science, with her desire to help others in a meaningful way. As a co-founder of Gen Z for BLM, Shaye plays a role in educating her peers on the issues that persist amongst marginalized groups today. She aims to help create safe spaces for BIPOC/LGBTQIA youth to share their voices and most importantly be heard.
