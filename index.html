Our mission as a youth-led organization is dedicated to shining a spotlight on pressing human rights issues that affect our society today. We achieve this by offering tutoring and mentoring services and actively collecting donations, all of which are channeled towards crucial causes. In particular, we stand in solidarity with organizations advocating for the Black Lives Matter movement, support nonprofits that are addressing the profound impacts of COVID-19 in hard-hit areas, and collaborate with charitable clinics to ensure healthcare access for uninsured and underinsured individuals in communities across the nation.
